Frequently Asked Questions

What is MedDocs?

It is an app which helps you to schedule your appointments and manage records easily along with medication reminders.

How secure is my medical data on MedDocs?

It is a very secure app and MedDocs will never share your information without your explicit consent.

How do medication reminders work?

Our medication reminder system allows you to set up custom notifications for each medication. You can configure dose amounts, frequency, time of day, and even track your medication adherence over time.

How do I update my profile information?

To update your profile, go to the 'Profile' section in the app. You can edit your personal details, contact information, and even upload or change your profile picture. These updates will be reflected in your account immediately.
